Ondemand Service

About

Ondemand app help the users to access major services available nearby. Users can search for services and book services at their covenience.Users can bulk book services for their home or offices at scheduled date and times.

My role

The project was an individual small budget project. This was one of my initial projects during the start of my career.

  • User Interface and Visual Style
  • Interactive Prototype
  • Frontend App Development
  • Backend Development
Tools and Technologies Used
  • Flutter
  • SOAP api for network calls
  • JSP for backend development
  • MySQL for database.
Challenge

Client was small business owner who wants his business presence online. The requirement was for an android application with minimal online shopping features.

Solution

From the client requirements, I understood app needs to stay within an affordable budget with must have features of an online shopping application but also be minimalistic.
